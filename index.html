This calculator should only be used by adults (pregnant or breastfeeding women should not rely on these BMI readings) and no action should be taken based on the value other than consulting a qualified person such as a doctor.

The calculator will give you an idea of how your weight compares to the general value. Body Mass Index (or BMI) is calculated as your weight (in kilograms) divided by the square of your height (in meters) or BMI = Kg/ms2.
